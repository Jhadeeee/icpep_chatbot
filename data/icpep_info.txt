[ABOUT ICPEP]
The Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines – Student Edition (ICpEP.SE)
is a national organization for Computer Engineering students in the Philippines.
Its main goal is to promote academic excellence, leadership, and camaraderie among CPE students.

[MISSION]
To develop globally competitive Computer Engineers through excellence in academics,
research, and community involvement.

[VISION]
A community of future Computer Engineers that uphold professionalism,
innovation, and collaboration for nation-building.

[FAQs]
Q: How to become an ICpEP member?
A: Fill out the membership form and pay the membership fee.

Q: What are the benefits of joining ICpEP.SE?
A: You gain access to seminars, workshops, competitions, and nationwide networking opportunities.

Q: Who can join?
A: Any student enrolled under the Computer Engineering program.

Q: How to contact ICpEP officers?
A: You can message the official Facebook page or visit the CPE department office.
